# Conversion Rate Formulas (Google Sheets)

## New Leads
=COUNTIF(Inquiry_Data!F:F, "New leads")

## Warm Leads
=COUNTIF(Inquiry_Data!F:F, "Warm Leads")

## Hot Leads
=COUNTIF(Inquiry_Data!F:F, "Hot Leads")

## Interested
=COUNTIF(Inquiry_Data!F:F, "Interested")

## Not Interested
=COUNTIF(Inquiry_Data!F:F, "Not Interested")

## Follow-Up
=COUNTIF(Inquiry_Data!F:F, "Follow-up")

## Total Leads
=COUNTA(Inquiry_Data!A:A)
